About us

Members of the Angolan Community in the Netherlands have come together to set up and implement this project.

Our mission is to relieve victims of extreme drought in Cuando Cubango, Angola.

Through this campaign, we want to donate clothing and food to combat the urgent need for basic foodstuffs.

We also want to bring the local government in Cuando Cubango into contact with Dutch experts in the field of water issues and agriculture.
